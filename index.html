story

Step into the role of Aurhea, heir to a fallen dynasty whose thirst for vengeance drives her to begin the ascent of a now-hostile kingdom. In this intense 2.5D platformer, string together perilous maneuvers, uncover the hidden side of Eukria and crystallize your enemies with your magical mace (aka your game controller) !
